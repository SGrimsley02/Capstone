What REMix does for you

REMix is a smart alarm and morning assistant that blends sleep science with personalization so your wake-up feels timely, useful, and low-friction.

Wake timing by sleep stage Triggers your alarm in an optimal window to reduce grogginess and help you start your day clearer.
Sleep-aware music playback Adapts your wake soundtrack based on sleep quality and your selected podcast tone preferences.
Daily podcast-style briefing Combines schedule, weather, news, and optional horoscope into a concise morning summary.
Connected routine Integrates with Google Calendar and Spotify so your morning context and media are ready immediately.
